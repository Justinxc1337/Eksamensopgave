PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

By navigating on Intecsystem.dk, you also accept our data policy. Intec System ApS is committed to protecting the personal information of our customers, partners, and others, and we take our responsibility for the security of your information very seriously. Therefore, below you can find information about the information we collect and how we use it.

We only collect personal data in cases where it is relevant to us, and we will only collect personal data if it is relevant to your activity at Intec System ApS. When collecting, processing, and using your personal data, we always comply with all relevant legal requirements.

We will only retain your personal data as long as we are legally obligated to do so or as long as it is relevant to the purpose for which it was collected.

Information We Collect

Intec System ApS collects and processes your personal data when you engage in the following:

  • Visiting our website or online webshop
  • Subscribing to our newsletter
  • Sending us questions, complaints, or job applications

We collect information such as cookies and similar technologies to improve your user experience on our website.

Furthermore, we provide non-personally identifiable information (completely anonymous data) to third-party applications such as Google Analytics, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

We collect personal data from you when you place orders on our webshop, create a webshop account, participate in a survey or contest, subscribe to our newsletter, or when you contact us.

In particular, we will collect information in the following categories:

  • Name, company name, VAT number, company address, email address, phone number
  • Information about your purchases on our webshop
  • Information about your use of our website
  • Location, including your computer's or device's real-time geographical location via GPS, Bluetooth, and your IP address, along with popular hotspots and cell tower locations if you use location-based features and enable location services settings on your device and computer.

We have entered into data processing agreements with our data processors, which is our guarantee that they comply with applicable rules for the protection of your personal information.

We also limit data access and processing to a very few authorized employees in a central, secure environment.
